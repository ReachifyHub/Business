# Offer: AI Receptionist Automation

## What's delivered

A WhatsApp/Telegram system that handles everything a human receptionist would for a small business:

- Answers incoming customer messages instantly, any time of day
- Answers FAQs (hours, pricing, services, location) using the business's own info
- Checks and communicates availability for bookings/appointments
- Takes bookings and confirms them
- Takes messages and details when a request needs the owner's personal attention
- Follows up automatically if a customer goes quiet mid-conversation

For Telegram specifically: built using Telegram's Chat Automation feature (Settings > Chat Automation), which connects a bot directly to the business owner's own profile so it can read and reply to their incoming messages on their behalf. Replies appear to come from their own account, not a separate bot — feels like the owner replying instantly, not a chatbot. No Telegram Premium required — available to any regular account, which means no extra cost or barrier for the client to use this.

## Pricing

£180 one-off setup + £60/month ongoing.

Setup covers: building and configuring the bot for their specific business (services, hours, FAQs, booking flow), connecting it to WhatsApp/Telegram, and testing before handover.

Monthly covers: hosting, AI running costs, and any adjustments as their business info changes. This is not optional or negotiable down to zero — it's what keeps the system running and maintained, not a one-time build left to rot.

These are starting numbers, adjustable per client based on scope (e.g. multiple locations, more complex booking logic costs more).

## Timeline

One week from agreement to live system, for a standard single-location setup.

## What this is NOT (keep pitches honest)

- Not a generic chatbot that only answers a fixed FAQ list — it handles real conversations
- Not a replacement for staff on complex judgment calls — it hands those off to the owner
- Not "AI that does everything automatically forever with zero input" — it needs their business info to set up correctly
