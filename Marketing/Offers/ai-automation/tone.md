# Tone: AI Automation Emails

Semi-formal, direct, competent. Sounds like someone who built the thing themselves and is confident it works — not someone selling a course about it.

## Sounds like this

"I noticed [Business] only lists a phone number for bookings — if that goes unanswered outside opening hours, that's a booking gone to a competitor. I build WhatsApp systems that pick that up automatically, day or night."

## Not like this

"Hi there! 👋 Are you tired of missing out on customers?? Our REVOLUTIONARY AI assistant will supercharge your business growth and unlock new revenue streams!!"

## Rules specific to this offer

- Never claim a specific number of "leads saved" or "revenue generated" without real client data to back it — until there's a first client, speak in terms of the mechanism ("catches messages you'd otherwise miss"), not invented outcomes.
- Say "I build" and "I run," not "we" — this is a solo operation and pretending otherwise undermines the direct, real-person credibility that's the actual edge here.
- It's fine to be plain about being new/solo if it comes up in a reply — frame it as "you're talking directly to the person who builds and maintains this, not a support queue," which is true and a genuine advantage over agencies.
