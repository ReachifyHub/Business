# Website DNA™ — Offer

## What's Delivered

A 7-chapter PDF ebook (~100–150 pages) with integrated worksheets, canvases, and an AI prompt library:

1. **Chapter 1: Why Websites Feel Generic** — Why AI-built websites all start to look the same, and why that's a context problem, not a tool problem. Sets up the whole system.
2. **Chapter 2: Business DNA** — Understanding the business's model, revenue, and operations before touching a single prompt.
3. **Chapter 3: Customer DNA** — Mapping who the business actually serves, their language, and their decision triggers.
4. **Chapter 4: Brand DNA** — Capturing the personality, voice, and visual identity that makes this business recognizable.
5. **Chapter 5: Content & Design DNA** — Translating brand and customer understanding into copy structure, layout logic, and design direction.
6. **Chapter 6: Feeding AI the Context** — How to use the completed DNA canvases as structured context for any AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Lovable, v0, etc.) — not a list of prompts, but a system for giving AI real business understanding.
7. **Chapter 7: Launch, Maintain & Grow** — Getting the site live with the right checks, then keeping it aligned with the business as both evolve.

Includes printable/fillable worksheets and canvases for each DNA layer, plus an AI prompt library built around the 7-layer system.
