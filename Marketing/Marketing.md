## Non-negotiables for every email this AI writes

1. Never mention age.
2. Always identify who I am and that this is a commercial email (UK PECR requirement).
3. Always include a working, one-line unsubscribe/opt-out.
4. One offer per email. Never pitch automation and ads in the same message.
5. Always reference something specific about the business being emailed — never a generic template with just the name swapped in.
